My Coaching Philosophy

I focus entirely on YOU

My Approach

My coaching is grounded in presence, curiosity, and trust. I work with every client on all levels — exploring patterns, beliefs, and behaviors that either support or limit them. I am here as maximum support, helping you uncover your potential and create the meaningful changes you truly desire.

Using Co-Active coaching principles combined with practical tools, we translate insight into action — aligning personal growth with professional performance and impact.

My Experience

With over 10 years of experience working with adults in international educational and business contexts, I've developed a deep sensitivity to communication, intuitive listening, emotional dynamics, and cultural nuance.

My background in pedagogy and specialization in working with children and individuals with developmental challenges shaped my empathetic, analytical, and intuitive approach to human behavior.

Over the years, I've worked with clients from Japan, China, Korea, and other parts of Asia. We worked on presentations, negotiation strategies, and complex business communication. Together, we clarified meaning, expressed thoughts and emotions, and explored the unspoken cultural norms.

With experience in international contexts, I bring cultural sensitivity, and deep respect for each client's unique path.

This progression led me organically to coaching. Where I continued my journey at Coaching Academy at NLP institute Slavica Squire. I completed the NLP Practitioner program and today I support professionals and teams in business communication, leadership presence, and personal development.

I focus on creating a space where the client can discover their own solutions. The best answers are the ones we uncover, not the ones we receive.

I know YOU already have it all. All it takes is the right question.
